The Best Movies According to Surina

My top 3 movies of all-time

Inception

This is my favourite science-fiction thriller. Christopher Nolan directed this film. He is a prodigious director who also directed the Batman trilogy.

Memoirs of a Geisha

This film tells the story of a Geisha in pre world-war 2 Japan. It's a compelling, yet heartwrenching story.

The Sound of Music

This an old, classic film with a beautiful story and music that makes you simply want to sing along in pure enjoyment.
